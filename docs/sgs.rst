Similar Grid Subdivision
========================

The Goldberg-Coxeter construction or Goldberg-Coxeter operation, following Stigler's law of eponymy, was discovered by Caspar and Klug.
https://arxiv.org/abs/1705.02848

* Geodesy: Geodesic grids and the quad sphere
